# AMI License Application — Step-by-Step Guide

**Source:** IMPIC (Instituto dos Mercados Públicos, do Imobiliário e da Construção)
**Application URL:** https://www.impic.pt/impic/acesso-a-atividade-da-mediacao-imobiliaria/pedido-online-de-licenca-de-mediacao-imobiliaria
**IMPIC Porto Office:** Available for in-person assistance

## What is the AMI License?

The **Licença de Mediação Imobiliária** is mandatory for anyone acting as a real estate mediator in Portugal. It's issued by IMPIC to **companies** (not individuals — agents work under a company's license).

## Two Application Paths

### Path A: Individual (Pessoa Singular)
If applying as an individual mediator (not recommended for our case — we have an Lda).

### Path B: Corporate Entity (Pessoa Coletiva) ← THIS IS OUR PATH
Applying through Bea's existing Lda (adding CAE 68311 — Real Estate Mediation).

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Add CAE 68311 to the Lda
- **Who:** Bea (company owner) + accountant
- **What:** Add CAE code 68311 (Mediação Imobiliária) to the existing Lda's business activities at the Tax Authority
- **How:** Submit Declaração de Alterações de Atividade via Portal das Finanças
- **Status:** ⏳ Pending — Phillip needs to ask accountant

### Step 2: Obtain Criminal Record Certificates
- **Who:** Every legal representative of the Lda (at minimum: Bea)
- **What:** Certificado do Registo Criminal
- **CRITICAL:** The purpose field MUST state **"Mediação Imobiliária"** — not generic
- **Where:** Order online at registocriminal online portal or in person at a Loja do Cidadão
- **Validity:** Must be valid at the date of AMI submission
- **Cost:** ~€5-10 per certificate

### Step 3: Obtain Civil Liability Insurance
- **What:** Insurance policy covering real estate mediation activity
- **Requirements:**
  - Covers real estate mediation (Mediação Imobiliária)
  - Territorial scope: Portugal
  - **Minimum coverage: €150,000**
- **Who to contact:** Any Portuguese insurance provider (Fidelidade, AXA, Allianz, etc.)
- **Estimated cost:** €500-1,500/year depending on provider
- **Also need:** Proof of payment (receipt or bank transfer confirmation)
- **Status:** ⏳ Need to get quotes

### Step 4: Prepare Corporate Documents
- **What:** Certidão Permanente do Registo Comercial (Permanent Commercial Registry Certificate)
  - Must have updated records: company name, HQ address, legal representatives
  - If there's been a street name change by municipality, need proof
- **Alternative:** Access code to Portal da Empresa
- **Where:** Portal da Empresa / Registo Comercial
- **Cost:** ~€10-20

### Step 5: Submit Online Application Form
- **URL:** [Online Application Form](https://www.impic.pt/RedDocPortal2021/App/Pages/Forms/FormPedidoMediacaoIngresso.aspx?ObjectClassName=LicMedImoIngresso&TipoRequerimento=LicMedImoIngresso&ANONYMOUS_Requerimento=true&Less=true)
- **What happens:** Fill out the form → IMPIC sends PDF documents via email
- **Important:** If data needs changing after submission, must start a NEW request (previous one is voided)
- **Cost:** IMPIC application fee (check current fee schedule)

### Step 6: Sign & Return Documents
- **What:** IMPIC emails PDF documents → legal representative(s) must sign them
- **Match:** Signature must match ID document
- **Submit:** Return signed documents + all other required documentation

### Step 7: Wait for Approval
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months from submission (with residency card in hand)
- **IMPIC reviews:** All documents, criminal records, insurance, corporate standing
- **If approved:** Lda receives AMI license number
- **Must display:** AMI number on ALL advertising, contracts, listings

## Prerequisites Checklist

Before starting the application:

- [ ] Residency card (Título de Residência) — required, not just visa
- [ ] CAE 68311 added to Lda
- [ ] Criminal record certificate(s) with "Mediação Imobiliária" purpose
- [ ] Civil liability insurance (€150,000+ coverage, Portugal scope)
- [ ] Permanent Commercial Registry Certificate
- [ ] Portuguese language ability (B1/B2) — needed for AMI exam (if applicable)

## Notes
- AMI license is issued to the COMPANY, not individuals
- Agents working under the company operate under the company's AMI
- No formal real estate education/exam required for the LICENSE itself (low barrier)
- But AMI exam (if applicable) is in Portuguese only
- Must renew/comprovar annually (Comprovação anual dos requisitos)
