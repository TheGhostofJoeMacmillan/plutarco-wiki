## 1. PURPOSE AND BACKGROUND

WHEREAS:
- The Referrer operates a marketing and content platform that connects English-speaking international buyers with real estate opportunities in Portugal;
- The Agency is a duly licensed real estate mediation company in Portugal, holding a valid AMI (Agência de Mediação Imobiliária) license issued by IMPIC;
- The Parties wish to establish a formal arrangement whereby the Referrer refers qualified international buyer leads to the Agency, and the Agency compensates the Referrer for successful referrals;
- The Parties expressly acknowledge that the Referrer is **not** acting as a real estate mediator, agent, or broker, and does not engage in any activity requiring an AMI license. The Referrer's role is limited to marketing, content creation, and lead introduction.

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 2.1 Referrer's Obligations
The Referrer shall:
(a) Create and maintain marketing content (website, social media, video, written guides) targeting English-speaking international buyers interested in Portuguese real estate;
(b) Identify and pre-qualify potential buyers through the Referrer's website and lead capture channels;
(c) Forward qualified leads to the Agency with the buyer's name, contact information, budget range, and property preferences;
(d) Clearly communicate to all referred buyers that the Agency is the licensed real estate mediator and that the Referrer does not represent either party in any transaction;
(e) Not engage in property showings, negotiations, contract preparation, or any activity that constitutes real estate mediation under Portuguese law.

### 2.2 Agency's Obligations
The Agency shall:
(a) Assign a dedicated English-speaking agent to handle referred leads;
(b) Contact referred leads within 48 hours of receiving the referral;
(c) Provide the Referrer with periodic updates on the status of referred leads (at minimum: monthly while active);
(d) Notify the Referrer within 5 business days when a referred lead completes or abandons a property transaction;
(e) Maintain its AMI license in good standing throughout the term of this Agreement;
(f) Ensure all referred buyers receive professional, licensed real estate services in compliance with Portuguese law.

### 3.2 What Constitutes a Completed Referral
A Referral Fee is earned when **all** of the following conditions are met:
(a) The Referrer submitted the lead in writing (email, form, or CRM record) with a date stamp;
(b) The referred buyer was not already a registered client of the Agency prior to the referral (see Section 3.4);
(c) The referred buyer completed a real estate purchase through the Agency;
(d) The Agency received its commission from the transaction.

### 3.3 Payment Terms
- Referral Fees are due within **30 days** of the Agency receiving its commission;
- All payments shall be made via bank transfer to the Referrer's account:
  - Bank: ___________________________________________
  - IBAN: ___________________________________________
  - Account Holder: ___________________________________________
- The Agency shall provide a statement of account with each payment, including the transaction address, sale price, commission earned, and Referral Fee calculation.

### 3.4 Lead Protection Period
- A referred lead is protected for **12 months** from the date of referral;
- If the referred buyer purchases any property through the Agency within this period, the Referral Fee is owed;
- If the same buyer is referred by multiple sources, the first documented referral date takes precedence;
- If the Agency can demonstrate the buyer was already in its active client database prior to the referral date, the Agency shall notify the Referrer within 7 days of receiving the referral, and no Referral Fee will apply to that lead.

### 3.5 Suggested Fee Range
For reference, typical referral fee structures in the Portuguese market range from **15% to 30% of the agency's commission**. For a standard 5% commission on a €400,000 property (€20,000 commission), a 20% referral fee would be **€4,000**. The Parties should agree on a structure that reflects the Referrer's lead quality and volume.

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in effect for an initial term of **12 months**. Thereafter, it shall automatically renew for successive 12-month terms unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days prior to the end of the current term.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience
Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time with **30 days** written notice. Termination shall not affect Referral Fees owed for leads referred prior to the termination date, provided the lead protection period (Section 3.4) has not expired.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:
(a) Breaches any material term of this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within 15 days of written notice;
(b) Loses its AMI license (Agency) or ceases operations;
(c) Engages in conduct that materially damages the reputation of the other Party;
(d) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

### 4.4 Lead Protection After Termination
Notwithstanding termination, the Referrer shall be entitled to Referral Fees for any referred lead that completes a transaction within the lead protection period, provided the referral was made before the termination date.

## 5. OBLIGATIONS OF GOOD FAITH

### 5.1 Non-Circumvention
The Agency shall not attempt to bypass the Referrer by directly soliciting referred buyers for future transactions without the Referrer's involvement, for the duration of the lead protection period. The Agency shall inform the Referrer if a previously referred buyer initiates a new transaction.

### 5.2 Exclusivity
This Agreement is **non-exclusive**. The Referrer may refer leads to other AMI-licensed agencies. The Agency may accept leads from other referral sources. Nothing in this Agreement restricts either Party from engaging in other business relationships.

### 5.3 No Agency Relationship
Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create a partnership, joint venture, employment, or agency relationship between the Parties. The Referrer is an independent contractor providing marketing and lead generation services. The Referrer has no authority to bind the Agency or make representations on its behalf.

## 6. COMPLIANCE AND LEGAL

### 6.1 Regulatory Compliance
Each Party is responsible for its own regulatory compliance:
(a) The Agency shall maintain its AMI license and comply with all Portuguese real estate regulations;
(b) The Referrer shall ensure its marketing activities comply with applicable laws, including truth-in-advertising, data protection (GDPR), and consumer protection regulations;
(c) The Referrer shall not represent itself as a licensed real estate agent or mediator in Portugal.

### 6.2 Data Protection
- The Referrer shall obtain consent from leads before sharing their personal data with the Agency, in compliance with GDPR;
- The Agency shall process referred lead data in compliance with GDPR and Portuguese data protection law;
- Both Parties shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.

### 6.3 Confidentiality
Both Parties shall keep confidential any proprietary information shared during the course of this Agreement, including but not limited to: lead data, pricing strategies, marketing plans, and client information. This obligation survives termination for a period of 3 years.

### 6.4 Intellectual Property
- The Referrer retains all rights to its marketing content, website, brand, and intellectual property;
- The Agency retains all rights to its property listings, contracts, and business materials;
- Each Party grants the other a limited, revocable license to use its name and logo for the purpose of marketing the referral relationship, subject to prior written approval of specific uses.

## 7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### 7.1 Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages. The total liability of either Party under this Agreement shall not exceed the total Referral Fees paid to the Referrer in the 12 months preceding the claim.

### 7.2 Indemnification
Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Party from any claims, damages, or losses arising from its own acts, omissions, or breaches of this Agreement.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 8.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **Portuguese Republic**.

### 8.2 Dispute Resolution
- The Parties shall first attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation for a period of 30 days;
- If unresolved, the Parties shall submit to mediation in Porto, Portugal;
- If mediation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Porto, Portugal.

### 8.3 Language
This Agreement is drafted in English. In the event of a dispute, the Portuguese translation shall prevail for legal proceedings.
