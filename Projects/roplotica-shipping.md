# Roplotica Shipping — Porto, Portugal Base

**Effective:** July 3, 2026

## Origin
All art ships from **Porto, Portugal** via CTT (Correios de Portugal).

## Shipping Service
**Correio Azul Internacional** — priority tracked delivery for goods up to 2kg.
- Europe: ~3 business days
- Rest of World: ~5 business days
- USA: ~5 business days (with customs formalities via Zonos)

## USA Customs Note
- US de minimis exemption ended Aug 29, 2025
- All goods shipments require Zonos pre-clearance (Declaration ID code)
- HS code 9701 (original artwork) is **duty-exempt** but still requires formalities
- CTT Correio Azul and CTT Expresso can ship to USA with proper customs documentation
- Recipient may be responsible for customs duties on non-art items
