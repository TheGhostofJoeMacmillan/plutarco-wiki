## Business Model (Phased)

### Phase 1: Content + Platform (Now — 3 months)
**Goal:** Build audience, brand, lead pipeline
- Curated listing site (manual curation → Idealista API later)
- Instagram: Porto real estate content (house tours, neighborhood guides, market analysis)
- YouTube: Longer-form video walkthroughs, "moving to Portugal" guides
- Newsletter/email list via website lead capture
- Revenue: $0 initially, building audience and email list

### Phase 2: Referral Partnerships (3-6 months)
**Goal:** Monetize through referral fees
- Partner with 2-3 AMI-licensed agencies in Porto
- Refer international buyers → receive finder's fee / referral commission
- Structure as marketing/consulting fees
- Revenue: €500-2,000/referral (negotiated)

### Phase 3: Licensed Agency (6-12 months)
**Goal:** Full legal operation as real estate agency
- AMI license obtained
- Direct listing representation
- Full commission structure (3-8% per transaction)
- Revenue: €5,000-20,000/transaction
