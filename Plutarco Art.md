## Medium
- **Primary:** Pen plotting — algorithms made physical through ink and machine
- **Surface:** Rusted Corten steel panels (11x14" landscape primary)
- **Technique:** Two-layer compositions — pen (mathematical) + paint (independent language)
- **Pens:** Micron 005 (0.20mm ultra-fine), Molotow 2mm (bold paint)
